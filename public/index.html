Features

Allot Room-Bed

This Feature in Hospital Management WebPage let the Hospital manage the entry of Patient. Which includes the following details of the Patient: Patient Name, Age of Patient, Aadhar Number, Contact Number, Date of entry, Type of Bed and also Discharge Time. This also shows a preview of the Patient Details on the same page after the entry of patient data in database and alot it the required type of bed. This software uses MongoDB a its Database.

Remove Room-Bed

This Feature in Hospital Management WebPage let the Hospital manage the Deletion of Patient. It found the Patient data associated with a particular name and contact number. This searches the patient data and return the data of that patient and shows its details on the second page. On pressing Discharge button on next page the Data will be removed from the current database and insert it in new collection in which details of discharged patient is stored along with thier discharge time.

Fetch Data of Patient

This Feature in Hospital Management WebPage let the Hospital manage the Deletion of Patient. It found the Patient data associated with a particular name and contact number. This searches the patient data and return the data of that patient and shows its details on the second page.
